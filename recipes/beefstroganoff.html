Beef Stroganoff

Description

This recipe is for beef stroganoff.

Ingredients

  • 1 package egg noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can fat free condensed cream
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Saut√© ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned.
  3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, 7 to 9 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. Drain and discard any fat from the cooked beef.
  5. Remove beef from the heat.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
