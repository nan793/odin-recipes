Lemon Asparagus Pasta

Description

This recipe is for lemon asparagus pasta, easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • 1 pound fresh asparagus
  • 1 large lemon
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water, bring to boil
  2. Stir asparagus into the pot with the boiling penne pasta and bring back to a boil; cook until pasta is tender and asparagus is softened but still bright green.
  3. Place penne and asparagus into a large bowl; lightly stir in lemon zest.
